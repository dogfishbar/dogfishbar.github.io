Though I've also taught several sections of
  • CSCI 1102 Computer Science 2

CSCI 3366 Programming Languages

This course focusses on the design, specification and implementation of modern programming languages. The course emphasizes the role of type systems in programming language design and implementation. Over the years, it evolved into a hybrid programming languages/compiler course.

CSCI 1103 Computer Science 1 Honors

This course has three themes: 1. to introduce the subject matter of computer science – computation, to help the student develop the skill of coding, and finally to introduce students to the curriculum of computer science as an academic discipline. The course uses the OCaml dialect of the ML programming language.
